The JMU Textbook Exchange

What is it?

The JMU Textbook Exchange is a service that allows users to buy and sell textbooks directly with one another. The goal is to remove the low sell back values offered by used textbook retailers by creating a medium for individuals to bargain and determine their own prices. Ultimately, we want students to save money.

How it Works

Users can choose to buy or sell textboooks. If one wishes to sell a textbook, they can navigate to the "Sell" page and create a post. Once a post is created it can then be accessed from the "Buy" page, this is where users search for existing textbooks to buy.
